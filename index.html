Higher Ground Institute

What is this?

A hands-on, 2-hour session where you'll build real projects with Claude Code and other AI tools. Perfect for beginners — we cap each session at 10 people so everyone gets support.

When we schedule a session we'll send the registration to this list — sign up when you see that come through because space will be limited!

Want to know more about adopting and learning about AI? Check out our Resource Guide!

What you'll need

  • A Claude (Anthropic) account — ideally a Max account if possible
  • The Claude desktop app on your laptop
  • Curiosity and willingness to learn!
  • Optional: A data set (csv, api, anything really!) you want to work with

How it works

  • 30 min — Setup & intros
  • 20 min — Project brainstorm & feedback
  • 40 min — Build time (with breakout help)
  • 30 min — Show & tell + reflection
